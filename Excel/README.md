## Bike Sales Dashboard

Here you'll find the dashboard I created in Excel to identify various insights and trends after data cleaning a global bike sales dataset. There is also a screenshot of the dashboard for quick viewing. 

## Tools/Skills Used
• Excel

• Pivot Tables

• Nested IFs

## Project Goal
Clean data ready for analysis and produce a dashboard reflecting insights & trends of global bike sales.

## Key Insights (Global)
• Customers with a salary of 55k+ were more likely to buy a bike. 

• The age group most likely to buy a bike was Adult. 

• Customers with a commute distance of 0-5 miles often bought bikes, after 5 miles most people did not.
